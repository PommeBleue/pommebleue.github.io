M1 Research internship

Research Internship done at the end of my first year of Masters (M1 Jacques Hadamard). It was supervised by Sonja Cox and Erwin Luesink. You can access my report here. During this internship, I worked on symplectic techniques for stochastic differential equations on symplectic manifolds and sketched a proof of the ergodicity of the invariant measures of Langevin Diffusions on compact Lie groups under the assumption of Hörmander's condition.
