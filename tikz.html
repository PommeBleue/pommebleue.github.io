PGF/TikZ figures

In here I'll keep a list of PGF/TikZ figures I've made and their code.

There is nothing really advanced in here, but I like the idea of keeping a list of the TikZ code for figures that I write. Making this list public is a plus, and it could also be of use for me whenever I need fast and easy access to one of these. Enjoy the list !
